About the conference

Very few disciplines have contributed to the making of nations, societies and the global community in a manner that the law has. Very rightly, lawyers are often called ‘social engineers.’ Often keeping their clients and their interests at the front, lawyers work hard day and night to fight for individual, civil and community rights. Add to that, developing economies in the recent years have undergone high growth in economic activities, leading to a large number of corporate entities. This has, in turn, led to numerous high-stake business transactions both internally and offshore, and the role of lawyers has become all the more complex and nuanced, both inside and outside courtrooms.

To give recognition to lawyers, legal teams, and firms that have carved a niche for themselves across various sub-areas of the law, Legitquest plans to host the First Annual “INDO-ASEAN Legal Conference & Excellence Awards 2018”. We will host 2-days event which will have various technical sessions on a variety of relevant themes for speakers to deliberate on the agenda and share knowledge. The second day will also have some more sessions followed by an awards ceremony. There would be various categories of awards which will be meant to reflect the extensive role that lawyers play in the justice system and the corporate world.

AWARD CATEGORIES

Recognizing Excellence in the area by legal firms and professionals from across the Indo-Asean countries- India, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines, Myanmar, Laos, Brunei, Vietnam and Cambodia, and Host nation India.
